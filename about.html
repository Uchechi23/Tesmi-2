About TESMI

Meet Us

TESMI was founded by Paramole Omotayo, the brand started off as a one man buisness in a shop in the streets of Lagos, Nigeria. Our brand came into the limelight and gained international recognition when the CEO of the company emerged as the winner of the Vlisco Fashion fund show which held in Holland in the year 2018. Ever since the great win, the Brand has only known growth and expansion as a result of the determination and the consistence of our Team. TESMI's studio is located in Lagos, Nigeria. It is an aesthetically pleasing studio and showroom, it is definitely a beauty to behold.

Our Founder

Paramole Omotayo, is the creative brain behind TESMI.
He is a student of the University of Lagos. He graduated from the department of creative arts in the faculty of arts. In the year 2018, Paramole Omotayo emerged the winner of the African's Fashion Funds hosted by the renowned, textile printing company, Vlisco in Holland. This win was a big one and definitely is the bedrock, he has successful built a strong team of designers willing to input the work for the growth of the brand.
